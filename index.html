About Me

Passionate About Clean Code & Design

Hello! I’m Gielang, a frontend developer based in Surabaya, Indonesia. I enjoy creating things that live on the internet, especially clean, responsive, and user-friendly web interfaces.

My interest in web development began in 2024, when I started exploring how websites are built and experimenting with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. What started as curiosity quickly turned into a strong interest in frontend development and modern web technologies.

Today, I focus on building personal projects and continuously sharpening my skills by practicing real-world layouts, improving responsiveness, and learning best practices in web development. I’m particularly interested in crafting accessible, well-structured, and visually appealing websites.

I’m currently looking for opportunities to learn, grow, and collaborate while turning ideas into meaningful digital experiences.
